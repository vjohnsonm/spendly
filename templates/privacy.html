Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the service, such as pages visited and actions taken.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain your personal expense tracking dashboard.
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Generate summaries and reports based on your own expense records.
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the service.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or unaffiliated third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password. We recommend using a strong, unique password and logging out when using shared devices.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use trusted third-party services to support operations, such as hosting infrastructure and web analytics. These providers are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

We use Google Fonts to load typefaces. This results in a request to Google's servers when you visit the site. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details on how font requests are handled.

We do not integrate with any external financial institutions, banks, or payment processors.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out through the support page. We aim to respond to all privacy-related requests within 7 business days.
